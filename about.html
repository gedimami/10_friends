About Friends !!

There is love in every true friendship. Good friends never give up on each other and always have their friend's back. There is mutual understanding and affection. They show love in the form of care. There is no jealousy between them, they always want the other person to be successful as much as they want themselves to be. Though some friends show love in the form of care and support some show it differently by teasing the other person in a funny way,etc. There is always the connection even if they haven't met for a long time. Love in friendship is one of the purest form of love
